How It Works



Using our Fake News Detection system is simple, intuitive, and fast. Here's how you can get started:


1. Begin at the Homepage On the main home screen of our website, you'll notice a button labeled "Let's Analyze". This is your starting point.

2. Redirect to the Prediction Page Upon clicking the "Let's Analyze" button, you'll be seamlessly redirected to our Prediction Page — this is where the analysis takes place.

3. Input the News Article Once on the prediction page, you'll see a large text box labeled for article input. Simply paste or type the news article you want to verify.

4. Click Analyze Below the text box, click the "Analyze" button. Our intelligent model will get to work instantly.

5. Get Your Result Within moments, the system will provide you with a clear and reliable result — indicating whether the news is likely real or fake.

Our tool leverages advanced machine learning models and linguistic analysis techniques to detect misleading or fabricated content with precision.

Stay informed. Stay empowered. Check before you believe — because truth deserves a second look.
